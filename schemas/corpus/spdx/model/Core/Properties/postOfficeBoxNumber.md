# postOfficeBoxNumber

## Summary

The number that identifies a PO box. A PO box is a box in a post office or other postal service location assigned to an organization where postal items may be kept.

## Description

A postal box (P.O. Box) is a secure, numbered mailbox located at a post office or mail facility, used to receive mail. It provides a fixed mailing address, independent of a physical home or business location.
