# EvidenceType

## Summary

EvidenceType refers to categories of documented or observable proof used to verify compliance, qualification, or performance

## Description

EvidenceType refers to categories of documented or observable proof used to verify compliance, qualification, or performance

## Metadata

- name: EvidenceType

## Entries

- report: Structured documentation of test results, inspections, or analyses.
- log: Time-stamped records capturing system or operational data recorded as usually as a response to specific triggers in a specified environment.
- recording: Captured datastream like audio, video, or any other kind of continuous electronic capture of events, behavior or conditions.
- observation: Documentation of direct monitoring or witnessing of the demonstration of processes, tests, or any kind of system responses during a specified timeframe under specified environmental conditions.
- other: Any other relevant type of proof or documentation not covered above.
