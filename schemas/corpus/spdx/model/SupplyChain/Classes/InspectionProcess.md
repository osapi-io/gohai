# InspectionProcess

## Summary

Inspection Process defines specific various processes needed to satisfy the inspection requirements for a specific product or service.

## Description

Different types of products and services require different types of inspection processes. Your specific inspection process is defined in this process. You can define multiple inspection processes for goods based on requirements.
