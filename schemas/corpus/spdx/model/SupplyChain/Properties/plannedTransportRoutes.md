# plannedTransportRoutes

## Summary

A transport route refers to the planned path or network used to move people, goods, data, or resources from one location to another.

## Description

A transport route is the planned path taken by people, products, goods, data or resources. Transport routes vary by product type, safety requirements, business constraints or other factors.
